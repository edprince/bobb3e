Group C5 - Lego Robot Game

The Game

The game we have designed is an adaptable maze which is solved by our robot 'Bobb3e', regardless of its arrangement.

Bobb3e's Capabilities

Bobb3e is capable of detecting any obstacles in his pathway throughout the maze, as well as presenting the user with a mini-game before picking up a box.

The Robot

Bobb3e (originally the official LEGO BOBB3E design) now uses 3 sensors. The original build only houses one, but we made modifications to Bobb3e, allowing him to carry an optical sensor and a touch sensor, either side of him, in order for him to operate correctly in the game.

The Score

The score continually increments, and the player aims to achieve the lowest score possible. Adding to this, the player can achieve a bonus for correct answers to the mini-game.
