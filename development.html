Group C5 - Lego Robot Game

The Robot

We decided to create a modified version of the 'Bobb3e' robot. This gives us a robot with tracks and a forklift, giving us the ability to pick up and reposition objects. It also included the infra-red sensor. We then added modifications to Bobb3e to include the optical sensor and the touch sensor.

The Game

Bobb3e solves an adaptable maze, in which he picks up an object and delivers it to the end. Also in the maze, the player must solve a maths-based mini-game. Once it reaches the end, he must deliver the object to the correct destination, depending on the input that the user gives him.

The Score

The score continually increments, and the player aims to achieve the lowest score possible. Adding to this, the player can achieve a bonus for correct answers to the mini-game, and is given time penalties for incorrect answers.
